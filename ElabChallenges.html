Challenges we Tackled

Together as a team, we worked on a total of six challenges. These challenges broadened our skillset, exposed us to hands-on problem solving and innovative thinking.

Challenge 1

Elevator Pitch

Our first challenge in E-lab was to present every Think-Tank member, what we like, a fun fact about us, what we bring to the table and a general reflection on what we are going to do in E-lab.

Challenge 2

Discover African

We told a unique story about an African community which was the The Kushites. We showcased their amazing cultures, addressed any misconceptions, highlighted their challenges, and emphasized their uniqueness through a podcast/stroy-telling documentary.

Challenge 3

Help Lab

For this Help-Lab challenge, we visited a school together as a team and shared learning resources with the staffs and the student. These resources were to help them understand English better as we observed that this was a challenge they faced.

Challenge 4

Help Lab

For this Help-Lab challenge, we visited a school together as a team and shared learning resources with the staffs and the student. These resources were to help them understand English better as we observed that this was a challenge they faced.

Challenge 5

Mission Voyage

In this challenge, we introduced ourselves, defined the problem we're solving, explained our mission, presented research findings, showcased our project idea, highlighted its impact, and demonstrated why we should receive the supposed ($10000) grant.

Challenge 6

EduEthics Digital Print

This website showcases all our processes in undergoing the six (6) challenges. It features our problem statement, mission statement, and our solution. It also features each member of the team and our collaborative approach to problem solving.
